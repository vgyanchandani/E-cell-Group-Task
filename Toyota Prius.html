Toyota Prius Latest Updates

The Toyota Prius is a large notchback hybrid that promises a drive experience similar to more powerful cars, yet a mileage comparable to a large hatchback. With the world going green, the Prius is Toyota's answer to help cut down on dependency on fossil fuels until a permanent solution comes up. It's a style statement as well.

OVERVIEW

July 1, 2017: With GST kicking in, prices of the Toyota Prius have been revised. Compared to thepre-GST price tag, the ex-showroom prices have gone up for all hybrids. Delhi: The Prius now costs Rs 45.23 lakh, as opposed to Rs 38.96 lakh pre-GST. That's an increase of Rs 6.27 lakh. Mumbai: The price of the hybrid car in Mumbai is now Rs 44.51 lakh, as opposed to Rs 43.76lakh pre-GST. That's an increase of Rs 75,000. Do note that prices for insurance are marginally higher, as they are taxed at 18per centinstead of the earlier 15 per cent.
