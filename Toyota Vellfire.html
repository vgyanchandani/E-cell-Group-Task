Since its India launch on February 26 2020, it retailed at a price tag of Rs Rs 79.50 lakh (ex-showroom India). The Vellfire MPV is a luxury 7-seater available in a single Executive Lounge variant. It is powered by a 2.5-litre petrol engine which makes 117PS of power and 198Nm of torque. It is helped by two motors each mounted at the front and rear axle which pitch in 142PS and 68PS of electric power, giving the Vellfire electronically governed all-wheel-drive. The hybrid system charges itself up using brake regeneration and gives this behemoth a claimed fuel efficiency of 16.35kmpl. Features include a 10-inch infotainment touchscreen with Android Auto and Apple CarPlay, a 13-inch rear touchscreen, tri-zone climate control, dual sunroof, cruise control, a 17-speaker JBL sound system, and 16-colour ambient lighting in the roof. Safety equipment consists of 7 airbags, ABS with EBD, electronic stability control, front and rear parking sensors, electric parking brake with auto-hold function as well as a tyre pressure monitor. Toyota also told us at the launch event that it has already sold around 180 Vellfires in India.

Engine and specification:

The Toyota Vellfire is powered by a 2.5-litre, four-cylinder, petrol engine paired with two electric motors. The combined power output is rated at 141bhp and 198Nm of torque. Transmission duties are handled by a CVT gearbox.

Exterior design:

Exterior design highlights of the Toyota Vellfire MPV include automatic LED headlamps, sequential turn indicators, fog lights with cornering function, dual sunroofs, and electrically adjustable ORVMs with heating and memory function.

Interior and features:

Inside, the Toyota Vellfire comes equipped with leather upholstery, powered captain seats for the second row, 16-colour ambient lighting, three-zone climate control, powered side doors, powered tail-gate, electronic parking brake, smart entry with push-button start, and a JBL-sourced 17 speaker music system.

Colours:

The Toyota Vellfire is offered in four colours including Burning Black, Pearl White, Black, and Graphite.

Rivals::

Rivals to the Toyota Vellfire include the likes of the Mercedes-Benz V-Class and the Kia Carnival.
